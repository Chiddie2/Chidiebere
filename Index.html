Dr. Chidiebere Emmanuel Okechukwu

Healthcare Scientist | Public Health Researcher | Fellow, RSPH

About

Dr. Chidiebere Emmanuel Okechukwu is a Nigerian-born healthcare scientist and public health researcher currently based in the United Kingdom. He specializes in cardiovascular diagnostics, cardiac rehabilitation, and public health epidemiology. He is a Fellow of the Royal Society for Public Health and currently serves at University Hospitals Plymouth NHS Trust.

Education

  • BSc in Human Physiology – Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Nigeria (2015), specialized in cardiovascular physiology
  • MSc in Physical Activity & Health Promotion – University of Rome Tor Vergata, Italy (2019), focused on cardiac rehabilitation
  • PhD in Public Health, Microbiology & Infectious Diseases – Sapienza University of Rome, Italy (2023), specialized in public health and epidemiology
  • PGCert in Clinical Echocardiography – University of the West of England, UK (June 2024)

Research Interests

  • Circadian rhythm and mental health in night-shift nurses
  • Cardiovascular imaging and echocardiography
  • Public health systems and infectious disease prevention
